More

With this HTML:


	<ul id="main">
	  <li class="item" >Item 1</li>
	  <li class="item" >Item 2</li>
	  <li class="item active" >Item 3</li>
	  <li class="item" >Item 4</li>
	  <li class="item" >Item 5</li>
	</ul>

We can find our active item like this:

$('#main .active')

Or the opposite:

$('#main li:not(.active)')
