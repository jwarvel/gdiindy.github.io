CHAPTER LEADERS

Lindsay Siovaila

Lindsay Siovaila is a Senior Solutions Developer for Salesforce where she builds demos using a variety of tools to help tell the customer story. She has 5 years experience in front-end web development, but most importantly, she is always learning. Outside of work, Lindsay is obsessed with bowling, decorating her old house and watching Lord of the Rings.

Lindsay believes she wouldn't be where she is today without the support from others throughout her life who encouraged her to pursue technology as a career. She hopes that bringing GDI to Indianapolis will open similar doors for others and give women in the community a place to encourage one another and build relationships while learning about the thrills of web development.

Erica Walker

Erica Walker is a Senior Software Quality Engineer at Salesforce, and is an Indiana native. She has worked in various roles in technology for the past 6 years, and is passionate about helping women as they learn how to code. Erica believes that the key to success in technology is working together, and trying new languages and platforms until you find what fits you. In her free time she is a cyclist, plays the ukulele, and enjoys hiking in the woods.

Virginie Adams

Virginie is a Project Manager at Salesforce Marketing Cloud and lives in Fishers, IN. When she heard about GDI through her friend Erica she got super excited to join in on such an amazing opportunity to help out girls how to code.

Before working at Salesforce, she studied Computer Engineering and Engineering Management at Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology. Being at an engineering school, she was always one of the few girls in the groups. After many years of trying to encourage girls to go into engineering GDI will be a great way to accomplish this goal.

Outside of work, Virginie enjoys shopping, watching Netflix, working on side programming projects to make sure her programming is not getting rusty and playing with her two dogs.
